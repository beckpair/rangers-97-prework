Why Do I Wanna Be a Software Engineer?

My dad has worked with computers my whole life, so it's something I've always been exposed to but never really done anything with. Since graduating, I've really struggled to find what I want to do as a career. I found through my work at escape rooms that I really, really enjoy solving puzzles. I also had the opportunity to program the light board for a production I was in a few years ago. Seeing the lights move and change colors simply because I typed in some numbers and pressed some buttons really gave me a dopamine burst. I mentioned this to my dad and he actually suggested programming to me, and now I'm here.

I would be remiss if I didn't attribute any interest in coding to my early Tumblr days as a 12 year old. I would spend hours messing around with the HTML code to make my blog look exactly the way I wanted, so, y'know.

Another aspect of this field I'm so drawn to is the ability to do this from anywhere and work remotely! I love to travel and want to do a lot of it in my life, so I feel like this kind of career would make that so accessible.
